Binomial Coefficient Simulator

About

Learn and simulate binomial coefficients with our interactive tool.

A binomial coefficient, often denoted as nCk or C(n, k), represents the number of ways to choose k items from a set of n distinct items. It's fundamentally important in various fields like probability, statistics, and combinatorics.

Formula:

  • nCk = n! / (k! * (n-k)!)
  • nCk = (n-1)C(k-1) + (n)C(k-1) (Pascal's Identity)

Our simulator provides a visual and interactive way to understand and explore these concepts.
